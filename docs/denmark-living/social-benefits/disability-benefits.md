# Disability Benefits and Support Services

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://borger.dk/handicap-og-sydom  
> **Applies to**: People with Disabilities and Chronic Conditions

## Overview

Denmark's **handicap- og sygdomsydelser** (disability benefits and support services) provide comprehensive assistance to people with **permanent disabilities**, **chronic conditions**, and **reduced work capacity**. The system emphasizes **inclusion**, **independence**, and **quality of life** through various financial benefits and support services.

## Key Information

- **Who is eligible**: People with documented disabilities or chronic conditions
- **When to apply**: As soon as disability affects daily life or work capacity
- **Prerequisites**: Medical documentation, residency requirements, assessment process
- **Processing time**: 3-6 months for initial assessment and decision

## Types of Disability Benefits

### Disability Pension (Førtidspension)

**For permanent work incapacity**:
- **Permanent inability** to work due to disability
- **Comprehensive assessment** of work capacity
- **Income replacement** for those unable to work
- **Available at any age** (no minimum age requirement)

### Resource Program (Ressourceforløb)

**Temporary support** for unclear work capacity:
- **Time-limited program** (up to 5 years)
- **Rehabilitation focus** and capacity development
- **Assessment period** to determine long-term options
- **Bridge to** employment or permanent benefits

### Senior Pension (Seniorpension)

**Early retirement** for reduced capacity:
- **Available from age 60**
- **Permanent work reduction** of at least 50%
- **Bridge benefit** until folkepension age
- **Medical assessment** required

## Eligibility and Assessment

### Medical Requirements

**Documentation needed**:
- **Comprehensive medical** records and diagnoses
- **Specialist assessments** and treatment history
- **Functional capacity** evaluations
- **Prognosis and** treatment possibilities

### Work Capacity Assessment

**Evaluation process**:
- **Multidisciplinary team** assessment
- **Physical and mental** capacity evaluation
- **Social and educational** background consideration
- **Rehabilitation potential** assessment

### Residency Requirements

**To qualify**:
- **Legal residence** in Denmark
- **Continuous residence** for specified periods
- **Integration into** Danish society
- **Future residence** expectations

## Application Process

### Initial Application

1. **Contact Municipality**
   - Visit local **rehabilitation team** or social services
   - Bring comprehensive **medical documentation**
   - Complete detailed **application forms**
   - Attend initial **assessment meeting**

2. **Required Documentation**
   - **Medical records** from all treating physicians
   - **Specialist reports** and test results
   - **Employment history** and work attempts
   - **Educational background** and qualifications
   - **Social circumstances** and support needs

3. **Assessment Process**
   - **Medical evaluation** by municipal team
   - **Work capacity** testing and evaluation
   - **Rehabilitation potential** assessment
   - **Decision and** benefit calculation

### Ongoing Reviews

**Regular assessments**:
- **Periodic reviews** of condition and capacity
- **Medical updates** and progress reports
- **Benefit adjustments** based on changes
- **Rehabilitation opportunities** evaluation

## Benefit Amounts and Calculation

### Disability Pension Rates

**Monthly amounts** (2025 rates):
- **Full disability pension**: Approximately 18,000-20,000 DKK
- **Partial disability pension**: Proportional amounts
- **Supplement for** severe disabilities
- **Additional support** for special needs

### Income Testing

**Benefit reductions** for:
- **Employment income** above threshold
- **Other pension** income
- **Capital income** from investments
- **Asset testing** for significant wealth

### Additional Allowances

**Supplementary support**:
- **Personal care** allowance
- **Mobility support** and transportation
- **Housing adaptations** and equipment
- **Companion allowance** for severe disabilities
