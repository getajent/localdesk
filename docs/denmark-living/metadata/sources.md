## Official Danish Government Sources

### Primary Sources

#### Borger.dk
- **URL**: https://www.borger.dk/
- **Language**: Danish (primary), some English content
- **Description**: Official Danish government portal for citizens and residents. Most comprehensive source for official procedures and requirements.
- **Authority**: Danish Agency for Digital Government (Digitaliseringsstyrelsen)
- **Usage**: Primary source for all official procedures, requirements, and regulations

#### Lifeindenmark.dk
- **URL**: https://lifeindenmark.borger.dk/
- **Language**: English
- **Description**: Official English-language portal for international citizens in Denmark
- **Authority**: Danish Agency for Digital Government
- **Usage**: English-language verification and supplementary information

#### Newtodenmark.dk
- **URL**: https://www.nyidanmark.dk/en-GB
- **Language**: English, Danish
- **Description**: Danish Immigration Service portal for newcomers
- **Authority**: Danish Immigration Service (Udlændingestyrelsen)
- **Usage**: Immigration, residence permits, and visa information

## Source Usage Guidelines

### Translation Priority
1. **Primary**: Danish-language content from borger.dk (most comprehensive and authoritative)
2. **Verification**: Cross-check with English versions on lifeindenmark.dk
3. **Supplementary**: Additional details from specific agency portals

### Source Conflicts
When sources provide conflicting information:
1. Prioritize borger.dk (Danish version) as most authoritative
2. Note discrepancies in documentation
3. Include reference to both sources
4. Indicate date of access for all sources

### Community Source Usage
- Use community resources to supplement official information with practical tips
- Clearly distinguish between official requirements and practical advice
- Verify community information against official sources when possible
- Indicate reliability level of community sources

### Citation Format
Each document includes:
- **Official Source** field in header with primary borger.dk URL
- **Official Resources** section at bottom with all relevant source links
- Inline references where specific information comes from non-primary sources
