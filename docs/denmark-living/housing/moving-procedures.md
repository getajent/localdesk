# Moving Procedures and Notice Periods

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://borger.dk/bolig-og-flytning/flytning-og-opsigelse  
> **Applies to**: All Tenants and Property Owners

## Overview

Danish **flytningsprocedurer** (moving procedures) involve **specific notice periods**, **legal requirements**, and **administrative tasks** that must be completed when **ending a tenancy** or **changing residence**. Understanding these procedures ensures **smooth transitions**, **deposit recovery**, and **compliance** with housing regulations.

## Key Information

- **Who needs this**: All tenants ending leases and anyone changing address
- **When to start**: Well in advance of intended move date (notice periods vary)
- **Prerequisites**: Valid rental agreement, proper documentation
- **Processing time**: Notice periods from 1-6 months depending on tenancy type

## Notice Periods and Termination

### Standard Rental Agreements

**Notice requirements for regular leases**:
- **Tenant notice**: 1 month to end of rental period
- **Landlord notice**: 3 months to end of rental period (with valid grounds)
- **Notice timing**: Must align with rental payment periods
- **Written notice**: Required in all cases with proper documentation

### Long-Term Tenancies

**Extended notice for established tenants**:
- **Tenancies over 3 years**: 3 months notice from tenant
- **Tenancies over 8 years**: 6 months notice from tenant
- **Protection against** arbitrary eviction
- **Landlord grounds** must be legally justified

### Fixed-Term Leases

**Temporary rental agreements**:
- **Automatic expiration** at end of agreed term
- **No notice required** for natural expiration
- **Early termination** requires mutual agreement or legal grounds
- **Renewal negotiations** before expiration date

### Student Housing

**Special rules for student accommodation**:
- **1 month notice** regardless of tenancy length
- **Academic year** considerations and timing
- **Summer sublet** arrangements and procedures
- **University coordination** for dormitory housing

## Giving Notice

### Written Notice Requirements

**Formal termination procedures**:
- **Written notice** (letter or email with confirmation)
- **Specific termination** date aligned with rental periods
- **Reason for** termination (if required by lease)
- **Delivery confirmation** (registered mail or receipt)
- **Keep copies** of all notice documentation

### Notice Delivery Methods

**Acceptable ways to serve notice**:
- **Registered mail** with delivery confirmation
- **Personal delivery** with signed receipt
- **Email** (if agreed in lease and confirmed)
- **Digital Post** through official government channels
- **Legal service** through courts (for disputes)

### Notice Content Requirements

**Essential information in termination notice**:
- **Tenant and landlord** identification
- **Property address** and rental unit details
- **Termination date** and move-out timeline
- **Reason for** termination (if applicable)
- **Contact information** for coordination

### Landlord Notice to Tenants

**Grounds for landlord termination**:
- **Non-payment** of rent or charges
- **Breach of lease** terms and conditions
- **Property sale** or owner occupancy
- **Major renovations** requiring vacant possession
- **Illegal activities** or serious misconduct

## Move-Out Procedures

### Property Inspection

**Final condition assessment**:
- **Joint inspection** with landlord or representative
- **Condition documentation** with photos and notes
- **Damage assessment** and responsibility determination
- **Repair estimates** and cost calculations
- **Cleaning standards** verification

### Cleaning Requirements

**Move-out cleaning obligations**:
- **Professional cleaning** standards expected
- **All rooms** including kitchen, bathroom, floors
- **Appliances** and fixtures cleaning
- **Windows** and outdoor areas (if applicable)
- **Carpet cleaning** or replacement if damaged

### Key Return and Access

**Final handover procedures**:
- **All keys** and access cards return
- **Mailbox keys** and building access
- **Garage or** parking space access
- **Final meter** readings for utilities
- **Forwarding address** for correspondence

### Utility Disconnection

**Service termination coordination**:
- **Electricity, gas,** water service termination
- **Internet and** cable service cancellation
- **Final bills** and account settlements
- **Meter readings** and usage calculations
- **Service transfer** to new address if needed

## Deposit Recovery

### Security Deposit Return

**Getting your deposit back**:
- **Timeline**: Typically 30 days after move-out
- **Deduction itemization** for any damages or cleaning
- **Dispute procedures** if disagreeing with deductions
- **Interest payment** on deposits held over one year
- **Bank transfer** to tenant's account

### Common Deposit Deductions

**Typical costs deducted from deposits**:
- **Damage repairs** beyond normal wear and tear
- **Professional cleaning** if not adequately done
- **Key replacement** if not returned
- **Unpaid rent** or utility bills
- **Lease violation** penalties and costs

### Disputing Deductions

**Challenging deposit withholding**:
- **Written dispute** with supporting evidence
- **Independent assessments** and repair estimates
- **Mediation services** for resolution
- **Housing court** proceedings if necessary
- **Legal representation** for significant amounts

## Address Change Procedures

### Official Address Registration

**Government notification requirements**:
- **Municipal registration** (folkeregister) within 5 days
- **CPR address** update through borger.dk or citizen service
- **Automatic notification** to government agencies
- **Voting registration** update for elections
- **Tax authority** notification for correspondence

### Service Provider Notifications

**Updating address with service providers**:
- **Bank and** financial institutions
- **Insurance companies** (home, car, health)
- **Employer and** payroll departments
- **Subscription services** and memberships
- **Healthcare providers** and GP registration

### Mail Forwarding

**Postal service arrangements**:
- **PostNord forwarding** service setup
- **Duration options** (3, 6, or 12 months)
- **Cost considerations** and payment
- **Digital Post** address update
- **Important correspondence** direct notification

## Cost Considerations

### Moving Expenses

**Typical costs for relocation**:
- **Moving company** services (5,000-20,000 DKK)
- **Vehicle rental** for DIY moves (500-2,000 DKK/day)
- **Packing materials** and supplies (500-2,000 DKK)
- **Utility deposits** and connection fees (1,000-5,000 DKK)
- **Address change** fees and administrative costs

### Tax Deductions

**Moving expense deductions**:
- **Job-related moves** over 50 km distance
- **Moving company** costs and transportation
- **Temporary accommodation** during transition
- **Documentation requirements** and receipts

## Timeline and Planning

### Pre-Move Planning (2-3 months ahead)

**Early preparation tasks**:
- **Housing search** and application processes
- **Notice giving** to current landlord
- **Moving company** research and booking
- **School transfers** and enrollment (if children)
- **Financial planning** and budget preparation

### One Month Before Move

**Final preparations**:
- **Address change** notifications to all parties
- **Utility arrangements** for new address
- **Packing preparation** and material gathering
- **Final inspections** and condition documentation
- **Moving day** logistics and coordination

### Moving Week

**Last-minute tasks**:
- **Final packing** and preparation
- **Utility meter** readings and final bills
- **Cleaning completion** and inspection
- **Key handover** and final walkthrough
- **New address** setup and services activation
