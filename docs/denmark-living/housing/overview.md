## Key Information

### Rental Costs
- **Deposit**: Typically 3 months' rent (refundable)
- **Prepaid rent**: Usually 1-3 months
- **Utilities**: Often separate from rent
- **Insurance**: Required by most landlords

### Tenant Rights
- **Rent control**: Limits on rent increases
- **Security of tenure**: Strong protection against eviction
- **Maintenance**: Landlord responsible for major repairs
- **Privacy**: Landlord must give notice for visits

### Notice Periods
- **Tenant**: Typically 3 months
- **Landlord**: 3-12 months depending on tenure
- **Written notice**: Must be in writing
- **End of month**: Usually ends on last day of month

## Common Questions

### How much deposit do I need?

Typically 3 months' rent as security deposit (depositum), plus 1-3 months' prepaid rent. Total upfront: 4-6 months' rent equivalent.

### What utilities do I need to set up?

Usually separate from rent:
- **Electricity**: Choose provider, set up account
- **Water**: Often included in rent or building fee
- **Heating**: Often included or separate bill
- **Internet**: Choose provider
- **TV license**: No longer required (abolished 2022)

### Do I need tenant insurance?

Yes, most landlords require indboforsikring (contents insurance). Covers:
- Personal belongings
- Liability for damage to rental
- Typically 500-1,500 DKK/year

### What is andelsbolig?

Cooperative housing where you buy shares in a housing association rather than owning the apartment outright. Benefits:
- Lower cost than ownership
- Security of tenure
- Participation in building decisions
- Can sell shares (with profit limits)

### How do I get my deposit back?

When moving out:
- Give proper notice (typically 3 months)
- Clean thoroughly
- Repair any damage beyond normal wear
- Attend move-out inspection
- Deposit returned within 1 month (minus any deductions)

### What if I have a dispute with my landlord?

Options:
1. Try to resolve directly
2. Contact Lejerbeskyttelse (tenant rights organization)
3. File complaint with Huslejenævn (rent tribunal)
4. Legal action (as last resort)

## Finding Housing

### Rental Platforms
- **Boligportal.dk**: Major rental platform
- **DBA.dk**: Classified ads
- **Facebook groups**: Local housing groups
- **University housing**: For students

### Waiting Lists
- **Boligselskaber**: Social housing associations
- **Long waits**: Can be years in popular areas
- **Register early**: Join waiting lists as soon as possible
