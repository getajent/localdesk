# SKAT Registration and Tax Authority Procedures

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://skat.dk/skat.aspx?oid=2244300  
> **Applies to**: All Taxpayers and Businesses in Denmark

## Overview

**SKAT registrering** (SKAT registration) establishes your relationship with Denmark's **tax authority** and enables access to **tax services**, **digital platforms**, and **compliance systems**. Proper registration is essential for **tax compliance**, **benefit eligibility**, and **business operations** in Denmark.

## Key Information

- **Who must register**: All residents, workers, and businesses with Danish tax obligations
- **When to register**: Upon arrival, starting work, or beginning business operations
- **Prerequisites**: CPR number (individuals) or CVR number (businesses), valid identification
- **Processing time**: Immediate for basic registration, 1-2 weeks for complex cases

- **Specialized departments** for different tax types and taxpayer categories

**Tax authority functions**:
- **Income tax** collection and administration
- **VAT and duties** collection
- **Tax return** processing and auditing
- **Taxpayer services** and guidance
- **Enforcement** and compliance activities

### Digital Services

**Online tax platform**:
- **TastSelv** (Do It Yourself) digital portal
- **Mobile apps** for tax services
- **Digital communication** and document submission
- **Real-time tax** information and calculations

## Individual Taxpayer Registration

### Automatic Registration

**When registration happens automatically**:
- **CPR number** assignment triggers SKAT registration
- **Employment start** with Danish employer
- **Residence establishment** in Denmark
- **Benefit applications** through government systems

### Manual Registration Requirements

**When you need to register actively**:
- **Self-employment** or business activities
- **Foreign income** reporting obligations
- **Complex tax** situations requiring special handling
- **Voluntary tax** services and programs

### Registration Process

**Steps for individual registration**:
1. **Obtain CPR number** from municipality
2. **Activate MitID** digital identity
3. **Access TastSelv** portal through borger.dk
4. **Complete tax** profile and preferences
5. **Set up digital** communication preferences

## Business Registration with SKAT

### CVR Number Requirement

**Business identification**:
- **CVR number** (Central Business Register) required for all businesses
- **Obtained through** Erhvervsstyrelsen (Danish Business Authority)
- **Links to** SKAT tax obligations automatically
- **Required before** starting business operations

### VAT Registration

**Value Added Tax registration**:
- **Mandatory** for businesses with turnover above 50,000 DKK
- **Voluntary** for smaller businesses
- **EU VAT** number for cross-border trade
- **Quarterly reporting** and payment obligations

### Employer Registration

**For businesses with employees**:
- **A-tax registration** for employee tax withholding
- **AM-bidrag** (labor market contribution) obligations
- **Pension contribution** reporting requirements
- **Annual reporting** of employee compensation

## Tax Card and Preliminary Tax

### Tax Card System (Skattekort)

**Preliminary tax determination**:
- **Automatic calculation** based on expected income
- **Employer withholding** guidance
- **Multiple tax cards** for multiple income sources
- **Adjustment during** year as circumstances change

### Tax Card Types

**Different card categories**:
- **Main card** (hovedkort) - primary income source
- **Secondary card** (bikort) - additional income (higher tax rate)
- **Pension card** (pensionskort) - retirement income
- **Foreign card** (udlandskort) - non-resident income

### Updating Tax Information

**When to adjust tax card**:
- **Income changes** (new job, salary increase/decrease)
- **Deduction changes** (pension contributions, mortgage)
- **Family changes** (marriage, divorce, children)
- **Moving** to different municipality

## Tax Compliance Obligations

### Annual Tax Return

**Yearly reporting requirements**:
- **Automatic pre-filling** for most taxpayers
- **Review and correction** of pre-filled information
- **Additional income** and deduction reporting
- **Submission deadline**: July 1 (extended for complex returns)

### Quarterly Reporting

**For self-employed and businesses**:
- **Preliminary tax** payments every quarter
- **VAT returns** and payments
- **Employee tax** withholding reports
- **Adjustment calculations** and payments

### Record Keeping Requirements

**Documentation obligations**:
- **Keep all** tax-related documents for 5 years
- **Digital storage** acceptable for most documents
- **Organized filing** system for easy retrieval
- **Audit preparation** and compliance verification

## Special Registration Situations

### Non-Resident Taxpayers

**Limited tax liability**:
- **Registration required** for Danish-source income
- **Withholding tax** procedures for some income types
- **Tax treaty** benefits and applications
- **Representative appointment** for complex cases

### Temporary Workers

**Short-term employment**:
- **Registration required** even for temporary work
- **Simplified procedures** for EU/EEA workers
- **Tax card** issuance for withholding
- **Final settlement** upon departure

### International Businesses

**Cross-border operations**:
- **Permanent establishment** registration requirements
- **Transfer pricing** documentation and reporting
- **International tax** coordination and treaties
- **Specialized advice** recommended for complex structures

## Penalties and Compliance

### Late Registration Penalties

**Consequences of delayed registration**:
- **Fines** for late business registration
- **Interest charges** on unpaid taxes
- **Compliance costs** and administrative burden
- **Audit risk** increase for non-compliant taxpayers

### Correction Procedures

**Fixing registration errors**:
- **Amendment requests** through TastSelv portal
- **Supporting documentation** for corrections
- **Professional assistance** for complex corrections
- **Voluntary disclosure** programs for serious errors

### Appeals Process

**Disputing SKAT decisions**:
- **Administrative appeal** to SKAT first
- **Landsskatteretten** (National Tax Tribunal) for formal appeals
- **Court system** for legal challenges
- **Legal representation** recommended for complex cases
