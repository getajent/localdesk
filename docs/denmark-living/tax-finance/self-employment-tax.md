# Self-Employment Tax and VAT Obligations

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://skat.dk/skat.aspx?oid=2244310  
> **Applies to**: Self-Employed Individuals and Business Owners

## Overview

Danish **selvst√¶ndig erhverv skat** (self-employment tax) encompasses **income tax**, **VAT obligations**, and **business compliance** requirements for entrepreneurs and freelancers. Understanding these obligations is crucial for **legal compliance**, **financial planning**, and **business success** in Denmark.

## Key Information

- **Who is affected**: Self-employed individuals, freelancers, and business owners
- **When obligations begin**: From first day of business operations
- **Prerequisites**: Business registration, CVR number, SKAT registration
- **Payment schedule**: Quarterly preliminary tax, annual final settlement

## Business Registration Requirements

### CVR Number Registration

**Central Business Register number**:
- **Mandatory** for all business activities
- **Obtained through** Erhvervsstyrelsen (Danish Business Authority)
- **Required before** starting operations
- **Links automatically** to SKAT tax obligations

### SKAT Business Registration

**Tax authority registration**:
- **Automatic** upon CVR number issuance
- **VAT registration** if turnover exceeds 50,000 DKK
- **Employer registration** if hiring employees
- **Specialized registrations** for specific business types

### Business Structure Options

**Legal entity choices**:
- **Sole proprietorship** (enkeltmandsvirksomhed) - simplest structure
- **Partnership** (interessentskab) - multiple owners
- **Limited company** (ApS/A/S) - separate legal entity
- **Branch office** (filial) - foreign company extension

## Income Tax for Self-Employed

### Business Income Calculation

**Taxable business profit**:
- **Revenue** from all business activities
- **Minus deductible** business expenses
- **Equals net** business income
- **Taxed as** personal income at regular rates

### Business Expense Deductions

**Allowable business costs**:
- **Office rent** and utilities (business portion)
- **Equipment and** supplies
- **Professional services** (legal, accounting, consulting)
- **Marketing and** advertising expenses
- **Travel and** transportation for business
- **Training and** professional development

### Home Office Deductions

**Working from home expenses**:
- **Dedicated workspace** requirement
- **Proportional utility** costs (electricity, heating, internet)
- **Office equipment** and furniture
- **Maximum deduction** limits apply
- **Documentation required** for space usage

### Quarterly Tax Payments

**Preliminary tax system**:
- **Estimated income** tax payments four times per year
- **Based on** projected annual income
- **Payment dates**: March 1, June 1, September 1, November 1
- **Adjustments allowed** during year as income changes

## VAT (Moms) Obligations

### VAT Registration Threshold

**When VAT registration is required**:
- **Turnover exceeds** 50,000 DKK in 12-month period
- **Voluntary registration** possible below threshold
- **EU sales** may require registration regardless of turnover
- **Service exports** to EU businesses

### VAT Rates (2025)

**Standard and reduced rates**:
- **Standard rate**: 25% on most goods and services
- **No reduced rates** in Denmark (unlike other EU countries)
- **Zero rate**: Exports outside EU, certain international services
- **Exempt services**: Healthcare, education, financial services

### VAT Return Filing

**Quarterly reporting requirements**:
- **Filing deadline**: 1 month and 10 days after quarter end
- **Digital submission** through TastSelv portal
- **Payment due** same date as filing deadline
- **Late filing** penalties and interest charges

### VAT Calculation and Records

**Input and output VAT**:
- **Output VAT**: Charged to customers on sales
- **Input VAT**: Paid on business purchases
- **Net VAT**: Output minus input VAT owed to SKAT
- **Detailed records** required for all transactions
