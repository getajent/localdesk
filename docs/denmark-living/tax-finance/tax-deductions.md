# Tax Deductions and Credits

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://skat.dk/skat.aspx?oid=2234820  
> **Applies to**: All Taxpayers in Denmark

## Overview

Danish **skattefradrag og kreditter** (tax deductions and credits) reduce your **taxable income** and **tax liability** through various allowances for **work-related expenses**, **personal circumstances**, and **approved investments**. Understanding available deductions can significantly **reduce your tax burden** and **increase your net income**.

## Key Information

- **Who can claim**: All taxpayers with qualifying expenses or circumstances
- **When to claim**: During annual tax return or through preliminary tax adjustments
- **Prerequisites**: Documentation and receipts for claimed deductions
- **Processing time**: Automatic for standard deductions, review for complex claims

## Standard Deductions

### Personal Allowance (Personfradrag)

**Basic tax-free amount**:
- **46,700 DKK** for 2025 (adjusted annually)
- **Automatic application** - no action required
- **Full amount** for residents
- **Proportional amount** for part-year residents

### Employment Deduction (Beskæftigelsesfradrag)

**Work-related standard deduction**:
- **10.65% of employment** income up to maximum
- **Maximum deduction**: 40,000 DKK annually
- **Automatic calculation** by SKAT
- **Covers general** work-related expenses

### Transportation Deduction (Befordringsfradrag)

**Commuting expense deduction**:
- **1.98 DKK per km** for distances over 24 km daily (2025)
- **Maximum 25,000 km** per year
- **Public transport** costs can be deducted instead
- **Documentation required** for distance and frequency

## Work-Related Deductions

### Professional Expenses (Faglige Udgifter)

**Job-related costs**:
- **Union membership** fees and professional associations
- **Professional literature** and subscriptions
- **Work-related courses** and training (not employer-paid)
- **Professional equipment** and tools
- **Home office** expenses (limited circumstances)

### Training and Education (Uddannelse)

**Skill development costs**:
- **Job-related training** courses and certifications
- **Professional development** programs
- **Language courses** for work purposes
- **Conference attendance** and professional events
- **Educational materials** and resources

### Work Equipment (Arbejdsudstyr)

**Tools and equipment**:
- **Computer and software** for work use
- **Professional clothing** and uniforms
- **Safety equipment** not provided by employer
- **Mobile phone** and internet (work portion)
- **Vehicle expenses** for work use

## Investment and Savings Deductions

### Pension Contributions (Pensionsindbetalinger)

**Retirement savings deductions**:
- **Ratepension contributions** - full deduction up to limits
- **Maximum deduction**: Varies by age and income
- **Employer pension** contributions reduce available deduction
- **Carry-forward** of unused deduction rights

### Investment Deductions (Investeringsfradrag)

**Capital investment incentives**:
- **Venture capital** investments in approved funds
- **Start-up investments** in qualifying companies
- **Green investment** schemes and bonds
- **Research and development** investment credits

### Charitable Donations (Velgørende Gaver)

**Approved charity contributions**:
- **Minimum 500 DKK** per organization per year
- **Maximum deduction**: 17,100 DKK annually
- **Approved organizations** only (SKAT list)
- **Documentation required** with receipts

## Personal and Family Deductions

### Child Care Costs (Børnepasning)

**Childcare expense deductions**:
- **Childcare institutions** and approved providers
- **After-school programs** and holiday care
- **Maximum deduction** per child varies by age
- **Income limits** may apply for full deduction

### Elderly Care (Ældrepleje)

**Care for elderly relatives**:
- **Home care** services for elderly parents
- **Nursing home** costs in some circumstances
- **Medical equipment** and accessibility modifications
- **Transportation** for care visits

### Disability-Related Expenses (Handicapfradrag)

**Disability support costs**:
- **Personal care** assistance
- **Special equipment** and modifications
- **Transportation** for disabled individuals
- **Guide dogs** and service animals

## Housing and Property Deductions

### Mortgage Interest (Rentefradrag)

**Home loan interest deduction**:
- **33.5% deduction** value for mortgage interest
- **Maximum deductible** debt varies by property type
- **Primary residence** and vacation homes
- **Automatic reporting** by banks to SKAT

### Property Maintenance (Boligforbedring)

**Home improvement deductions**:
- **Craftsman services** (håndværkerfradrag) - 25% of labor costs
- **Maximum 25,000 DKK** deduction per household
- **Green improvements** may have enhanced deductions
- **Documentation required** with invoices

### Energy Improvements (Energiforbedringer)

**Environmental upgrade incentives**:
- **Solar panels** and renewable energy systems
- **Insulation** and energy efficiency improvements
- **Heat pumps** and efficient heating systems
- **Electric vehicle** charging stations

## Health and Medical Deductions

### Medical Expenses (Sundhedsudgifter)

**Healthcare cost deductions**:
- **Prescription medications** above threshold
- **Dental treatment** and orthodontics
- **Physiotherapy** and rehabilitation
- **Medical equipment** and aids
- **Threshold amount** must be exceeded for deduction

### Health Insurance (Sundhedsforsikring)

**Private health insurance premiums**:
- **Supplementary insurance** premiums
- **Dental insurance** costs
- **Critical illness** insurance
- **Income limits** may apply

## Special Circumstances Deductions

### Moving Expenses (Flytning)

**Relocation cost deductions**:
- **Job-related moves** over 50 km
- **Moving company** costs and transportation
- **Temporary accommodation** during transition
- **Double housing** costs for limited period

### Divorce and Separation (Skilsmisse)

**Legal and related costs**:
- **Legal fees** for divorce proceedings
- **Mediation** and counseling costs
- **Child custody** legal expenses
- **Property division** professional fees

### Unemployment Costs (Arbejdsløshed)

**Job search expenses**:
- **Job application** costs and materials
- **Interview travel** expenses
- **Career counseling** and coaching
- **Networking events** and job fairs

## Documentation Requirements

### Receipt and Record Keeping

**Required documentation**:
- **Original receipts** for all claimed expenses
- **Bank statements** showing payments
- **Contracts and** agreements for services
- **Mileage logs** for transportation deductions
- **Professional certificates** for work-related training

### Digital Documentation

**Electronic record keeping**:
- **Digital receipts** accepted for most deductions
- **Photo documentation** of paper receipts
- **Cloud storage** for backup and organization
- **SKAT digital** submission systems

### Audit Preparation

**In case of review**:
- **Organized filing** system for all tax documents
- **Clear categorization** of different deduction types
- **Supporting documentation** for unusual or large deductions
- **Professional advice** for complex situations

## Claiming Deductions

### Automatic Deductions

**No action required**:
- **Personal allowance** and employment deduction
- **Mortgage interest** (reported by banks)
- **Pension contributions** (reported by employers)
- **Standard transportation** (if registered with employer)

### Manual Claims

**Requires active reporting**:
- **Professional expenses** and training costs
- **Charitable donations** and investments
- **Medical expenses** above threshold
- **Home improvement** and craftsman services

### Timing of Claims

**When to report deductions**:
- **During tax return** preparation (March-July)
- **Preliminary tax** adjustments during the year
- **Amended returns** for missed deductions (within deadlines)
- **Carry-forward** for multi-year deductions

## Common Mistakes and Pitfalls

### Documentation Errors

**Frequent problems**:
- **Missing receipts** or inadequate documentation
- **Personal vs. business** expense confusion
- **Double claiming** of employer-reimbursed expenses
- **Incorrect categorization** of deduction types

### Timing Issues

**Deadline and period errors**:
- **Wrong tax year** for expense claims
- **Missing deadlines** for deduction claims
- **Premature claiming** of multi-year expenses
- **Carry-forward** rule misunderstandings

### Eligibility Mistakes

**Common qualification errors**:
- **Income limits** for certain deductions
- **Minimum thresholds** not met
- **Unapproved organizations** for charitable donations
- **Personal use** portion of business expenses
