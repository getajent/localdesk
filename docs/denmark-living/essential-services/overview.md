## Quick Reference

### Healthcare
- **Emergency**: Call 112
- **GP**: First point of contact for medical issues
- **Hospitals**: Free emergency and specialist care
- **Prescriptions**: Subsidized through GP

### Banking
- **NemKonto**: Required for public payments
- **MobilePay**: Popular payment app
- **Cards**: Dankort widely accepted
- **Online Banking**: Requires MitID

### Digital Services
- **Borger.dk**: Main government portal
- **Digital Post**: Mandatory for official mail
- **MitID**: Access to all digital services
- **E-Boks**: Digital mailbox

### Education
- **Daycare**: Available from age 0-6
- **School**: Mandatory ages 6-16
- **Free**: Public schools and daycare (with fees)
- **International**: Options available

### Transportation
- **Rejsekort**: Travel card for public transit
- **Bikes**: Extensive cycling infrastructure
- **Trains**: DSB national railway
- **Buses**: Local and regional services
