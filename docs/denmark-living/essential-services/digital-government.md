# Digital Government Services

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://borger.dk/internet-og-telefoni/digitale-tjenester  
> **Applies to**: All Residents

## Overview

Denmark's **digitale offentlige tjenester** (digital government services) provide comprehensive online access to government services, applications, and information. The system is built around **MitID** authentication and **borger.dk** portal, enabling residents to handle most government interactions digitally from home.

## Key Information

- **Who can use**: All residents with CPR number and MitID
- **When available**: 24/7 online access to most services
- **Prerequisites**: CPR number, MitID digital identity, internet access
- **Processing time**: Instant for information, varies for applications

## Core Digital Infrastructure

### MitID Authentication

**MitID** serves as the **digital key** to all government services:
- **Secure login** to all government portals
- **Digital signature** for official documents
- **Identity verification** for sensitive transactions
- **Multi-factor authentication** for enhanced security

### Borger.dk Portal

**Central government portal** providing:
- **Single access point** to all government services
- **Personal dashboard** with relevant information
- **Application tracking** and status updates
- **Document storage** and retrieval
- **Service recommendations** based on your situation
