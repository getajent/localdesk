# Community Resources and Integration Support

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://borger.dk/integration-og-sociale-forhold  
> **Applies to**: All Residents, Especially Newcomers

## Overview

Denmark offers extensive **samfundsressourcer og integrationsstøtte** (community resources and integration support) to help residents **connect**, **integrate**, and **thrive** in Danish society. These resources include **government programs**, **volunteer organizations**, **cultural associations**, and **community centers** that provide practical assistance and social connections.

## Key Information

- **Who can access**: All residents, with some programs specifically for newcomers
- **When available**: Year-round services with varying schedules
- **Prerequisites**: Generally open access, some programs require registration
- **Cost**: Many services free or low-cost, some fee-based programs
