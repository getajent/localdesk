# Mobile Phone and Internet Services

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://borger.dk/internet-og-telefoni  
> **Applies to**: All Residents

## Overview

Denmark's **mobil- og internetudbydere** (mobile and internet providers) offer comprehensive **telecommunications services** with excellent **coverage**, **high speeds**, and **competitive pricing**. The market features both **major carriers** and **budget alternatives**, providing options for all needs and budgets.

## Key Information

- **Who can get service**: All residents with valid identification and address
- **When to apply**: Immediately upon arrival or as needed
- **Prerequisites**: CPR number, Danish address, identification, credit check
- **Processing time**: Same-day activation for most services

## Mobile Service Plans

### Prepaid Plans

**Pay-as-you-go options**:
- **No monthly** commitment or contract
- **Top-up cards** available at stores
- **Good for** temporary residents or light users
- **Typical costs**: 50-200 DKK for monthly packages
- **Data allowances**: 1-20 GB depending on price

### Postpaid Plans

**Monthly subscription services**:
- **Monthly billing** and automatic payment
- **Higher data** allowances and better rates
- **International calling** and roaming included
- **Typical costs**: 100-500 DKK per month
- **Unlimited calling** and texting within Denmark

### Family Plans

**Multi-line discounts**:
- **Shared data** pools across multiple lines
- **Discounted rates** for additional lines
- **Centralized billing** and management
- **Parental controls** and usage monitoring
- **Flexible individual** line customization

### Business Plans

**Commercial and enterprise services**:
- **Volume discounts** for multiple lines
- **Advanced features** (mobile device management)
- **Priority support** and service levels
- **International services** and global roaming
- **Expense management** and reporting tools

## Internet Service Providers

### Fiber Internet (Fiber)

**High-speed fiber optic connections**:
- **Speeds up to** 1 Gbps or higher
- **Most reliable** and fastest option
- **Available in** most urban areas and many rural locations
- **Typical costs**: 200-600 DKK per month
- **Installation** may require technician visit

### Cable Internet (Kabel)

**Cable TV network internet**:
- **High speeds** up to 500 Mbps
- **Widely available** in urban and suburban areas
- **Often bundled** with TV and phone services
- **Typical costs**: 150-400 DKK per month
- **Shared bandwidth** may affect peak speeds

### DSL Internet

**Digital subscriber line over phone lines**:
- **Lower speeds** but widely available
- **Good for** basic internet needs
- **Rural area** availability where fiber not present
- **Typical costs**: 100-300 DKK per month
- **Speed depends** on distance from exchange

### Mobile Internet

**4G/5G wireless internet**:
- **Portable hotspots** and mobile routers
- **Good backup** or temporary solution
- **Data limits** and speed throttling
- **Typical costs**: 200-500 DKK per month
- **Coverage dependent** on mobile network
