# Arrival Process - Overview

> **Last Updated**: 2025-01-15  
> **Official Source**: https://lifeindenmark.borger.dk/  
> **Applies to**: All new residents

## Overview

When you arrive in Denmark, there are several essential registrations and procedures you must complete to access services, work legally, and integrate into Danish society. This guide provides a prioritized checklist and overview of the arrival process.

## Priority Timeline

### Week 1-2: Immediate Priorities
1. **Find temporary accommodation** (if not arranged)
2. **Get a Danish SIM card** for local phone number
3. **Open a bank account** (if possible without CPR)
4. **Register with ICS** (International Citizen Service) for guidance

### Month 1: Essential Registrations
1. **Apply for CPR number** (requires permanent address and 3-month stay proof)
2. **Register for MitID** (requires CPR number)
3. **Apply for health insurance card** (yellow card)
4. **Register with a GP** (family doctor)
5. **Get tax card** (skattekort)

### Month 2-3: Complete Setup
1. **Set up NemKonto** (for receiving public payments)
2. **Register for Digital Post**
3. **Import vehicle** (if applicable) - 30-day deadline
4. **Exchange driving license** (if applicable)
5. **Enroll in Danish language courses**

## Essential Registrations

### [CPR Number](cpr-number.md)
**Priority**: Critical  
**Timeline**: Apply as soon as you have permanent address  
**Why**: Required for almost all other services including healthcare, banking, employment, and benefits

**Quick Facts**:
- 10-digit personal identification number
- Requires proof of 3-month minimum stay
- Requires permanent address
- Processing time: Usually same-day appointment

### [MitID](mitid.md)
**Priority**: Critical  
**Timeline**: Register immediately after getting CPR number  
**Why**: Digital identity for accessing government services, banking, and many private services

**Quick Facts**:
- Replaces old NemID system
- Requires CPR number
- Can be set up online or at citizen service center
- Essential for online banking and government portals

### [Health Insurance Card](health-insurance.md)
**Priority**: High  
**Timeline**: Apply after CPR registration  
**Why**: Proof of healthcare coverage, needed for accessing medical services

**Quick Facts**:
- Yellow card (sundhedskort)
- Automatic coverage after CPR registration
- Card arrives by mail
- Temporary coverage available immediately

### [GP Registration](gp-registration.md)
**Priority**: High  
**Timeline**: Register within first month  
**Why**: Required for accessing healthcare system, GP is your first point of contact for medical issues

**Quick Facts**:
- Choose from GPs in your area
- Register online or by phone
- Free service
- Required for prescriptions and specialist referrals

### [Tax Card](tax-card.md)
**Priority**: High (if employed)  
**Timeline**: Before starting work  
**Why**: Tells employer how much tax to withhold from salary

**Quick Facts**:
- Main card (hovedkort) for primary job
- Secondary card (bikort) for additional income
- Set up through SKAT website
- Employer needs your tax card information

### [Bank Account](bank-account.md)
**Priority**: High  
**Timeline**: As soon as possible, ideally with CPR number  
**Why**: Needed for salary, paying bills, and receiving public payments

**Quick Facts**:
- Some banks allow accounts without CPR (limited services)
- Full account access requires CPR number
- Set up NemKonto for receiving public payments
- Bring passport, residence permit, and proof of address

### [Digital Post](digital-post.md)
**Priority**: Medium  
**Timeline**: Set up within first 2 months  
**Why**: Mandatory system for receiving official correspondence from government and many companies

**Quick Facts**:
- Replaces physical mail for official communications
- Requires MitID to access
- Check regularly for important notices
- Can opt out only in limited circumstances

### [Vehicle Import](vehicle-import.md)
**Priority**: High (if bringing vehicle)  
**Timeline**: Must register within 30 days of arrival  
**Why**: Legal requirement, significant penalties for late registration

**Quick Facts**:
- 30-day deadline from arrival
- Registration tax can be substantial
- Requires vehicle inspection
- Danish license plates required

### [ICS Centers](ics-centers.md)
**Priority**: Medium  
**Timeline**: Visit early for guidance  
**Why**: Provides assistance with registrations and integration

**Quick Facts**:
- International Citizen Service centers
- Help with CPR, permits, and procedures
- Book appointment online
- Free service

### [Language Courses](language-courses.md)
**Priority**: Medium  
**Timeline**: Enroll within first 3 months  
**Why**: Danish language essential for integration and many job opportunities

**Quick Facts**:
- Free or subsidized courses available
- Different levels based on background
- Some courses mandatory for certain residence permits
- Evening and daytime options

## Required Documents Checklist

Bring these documents to Denmark and keep them accessible:

### Essential Documents
- ✓ Valid passport
- ✓ Residence permit (non-EU citizens)
- ✓ Birth certificate (original or certified copy)
- ✓ Marriage certificate (if applicable)
- ✓ Educational certificates and diplomas
- ✓ Employment contract or job offer letter
- ✓ Proof of accommodation (rental contract or letter from host)

### Financial Documents
- ✓ Bank statements (last 3 months)
- ✓ Proof of funds for initial period
- ✓ International payment cards

### For Families
- ✓ Children's birth certificates
- ✓ Custody documents (if applicable)
- ✓ School records and vaccination records
- ✓ Marriage certificate

### Additional Useful Documents
- ✓ Driving license (for exchange)
- ✓ Vehicle registration (if importing car)
- ✓ Medical records and prescriptions
- ✓ Professional certifications
- ✓ Reference letters from previous employers

## Important Deadlines

| Deadline | Requirement | Consequence of Missing |
|----------|-------------|----------------------|
| 30 days | Vehicle registration | Fines and penalties |
| 3 months | EU residence registration | May affect right to stay |
| Before work starts | Tax card | Emergency tax rate (higher) |
| ASAP | CPR number | Cannot access services |

## Common Questions

### What should I do first when I arrive?

1. Secure accommodation (even temporary)
2. Get a Danish phone number
3. Visit ICS center for guidance
4. Apply for CPR number as soon as you have permanent address

### Can I work before getting a CPR number?

Technically yes if you have work authorization, but it's very difficult. Most employers require CPR number for payroll. Get your CPR number as quickly as possible.

### How long does it take to complete all registrations?

- **CPR number**: Same day (with appointment)
- **MitID**: Same day (after CPR)
- **Health card**: Arrives within 1-2 weeks
- **GP registration**: Immediate
- **Tax card**: Immediate (online)
- **Bank account**: Same day to 1 week
- **Digital Post**: Immediate (after MitID)

Total timeline: Most essential registrations can be completed within 2-4 weeks if you have permanent address.

### What if I don't have permanent accommodation yet?

You cannot get a CPR number without a permanent address. Options:
- Stay in temporary accommodation while searching
- Some landlords allow registration before move-in
- Consider short-term rental with registration possibility
- See [Housing Search](../before-moving/housing-search.md) for strategies

### Do I need to do these in a specific order?

Yes, there are dependencies:
1. **Permanent address** → CPR number
2. **CPR number** → MitID, health card, GP registration, full bank account
3. **MitID** → Digital Post, online government services
4. **Tax card** → Can be done with CPR number, needed before work

## Getting Help

### International Citizen Service (ICS)
- Specialized help for international citizens
- Book appointments online
- Assistance with registrations
- See [ICS Centers](ics-centers.md) for locations

### Citizen Service Centers (Borgerservice)
- Local municipality services
- CPR registration
- Various administrative services
- Find your local center on borger.dk
