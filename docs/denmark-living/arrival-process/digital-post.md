# Digital Post (Digital Government Communication)

> **Last Updated**: 2025-02-03  
> **Official Source**: https://borger.dk/internet-og-telefoni/digital-post  
> **Applies to**: All Residents

## Overview

**Digital Post** is Denmark's mandatory digital communication system for receiving official correspondence from government agencies, municipalities, and many private companies. All residents with a **CPR-nummer** (civil registration number) are automatically enrolled and must use this system to receive important documents, tax information, and official notices.

## Key Information

- **Who needs this**: All residents with CPR number (mandatory enrollment)
- **When to activate**: Immediately after receiving CPR number and MitID
- **Prerequisites**: CPR number, MitID digital identity, email address
- **Processing time**: Immediate activation, messages arrive instantly

## What is Digital Post

### Mandatory System

Digital Post is **required by law** for:
- All residents aged 15 and older
- Communication with government agencies
- Tax authorities (SKAT)
- Municipal services
- Healthcare system
- Many private companies

### Types of Messages

You will receive:
- **Tax documents** (årsopgørelse, tax cards)
- **Healthcare information** (appointment confirmations, test results)
- **Municipal notices** (waste collection, local services)
- **Benefits information** (unemployment, housing support)
- **Legal documents** (court notices, official decisions)
- **Banking correspondence** (loan documents, account information)

## Setting Up Digital Post

### Automatic Enrollment

- **Automatic**: Enrolled when you receive CPR number
- **Activation**: Must activate through borger.dk with MitID
- **Email notifications**: Set up to receive alerts about new messages

### Activation Steps

1. **Access borger.dk**
   - Go to borger.dk
   - Log in with **MitID**
   - Navigate to "Digital Post" section

2. **Activate Service**
   - Confirm your contact information
   - Set email notification preferences
   - Choose message delivery settings
   - Accept terms and conditions

3. **Configure Notifications**
   - **Email alerts**: Get notified of new messages
   - **SMS alerts**: Optional text message notifications
   - **Frequency**: Choose immediate or daily digest
   - **Language**: Set preferred language for notifications

### Required Information

- Valid CPR number
- MitID digital identity
- Email address for notifications
- Mobile phone number (optional for SMS alerts)

## Using Digital Post

### Accessing Messages

1. **Through borger.dk**
   - Log in with MitID
   - Click "Digital Post" or "Min Post"
   - View all messages in chronological order

2. **Through Mobile App**
   - Download "Mit Borger.dk" app
   - Log in with MitID
   - Access messages on mobile device

### Message Management

- **Reading**: Click to open and read messages
- **Downloading**: Save important documents as PDF
- **Archiving**: Organize messages in folders
- **Searching**: Find specific messages by sender or date
- **Printing**: Print documents when needed

### Message Retention

- **Storage period**: Messages kept for 10 years
- **Automatic deletion**: Old messages removed after retention period
- **Download important documents**: Save copies of crucial documents

## Exemptions and Alternatives

### Who Can Be Exempt

Limited exemptions available for:
- **Severe digital illiteracy** (with documentation)
- **Cognitive impairments** (with medical documentation)
- **No internet access** (rare, requires justification)

### Applying for Exemption

1. Contact your **municipality's citizen service center**
2. Provide documentation of inability to use digital services
3. Complete exemption application form
4. Receive decision (exemptions rarely granted)

### Alternative for Exempt Individuals

- **Physical mail**: Receive paper letters by post
- **Higher costs**: May be charged for paper delivery
- **Limited services**: Some services only available digitally

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Access Digital Post

**Common solutions**:
- Ensure MitID is working properly
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try different browser or device
- Contact borger.dk support

### Missing Messages

**Check these factors**:
- Correct CPR number registered
- Messages may be in spam/junk folder (for email notifications)
- Some senders may still use physical mail
- Contact sender directly if expecting specific document

### Technical Problems

**Get help from**:
- **Borger.dk support**: Online chat or phone support
- **Local library**: Many offer digital assistance
- **Municipality**: Citizen service centers provide help
- **Digital support centers**: Available in most cities

## Important Deadlines

### Response Requirements

- **Tax documents**: Must respond within specified deadlines
- **Legal notices**: Response times vary, check each document
- **Municipal notices**: Follow deadlines for appeals or responses
- **Healthcare**: Respond to appointment requests promptly

### Consequences of Not Using

- **Legal notices**: May be considered "delivered" even if not read
- **Tax deadlines**: Missing deadlines can result in penalties
- **Benefits**: May lose eligibility if not responding to requests
- **Healthcare**: May miss important appointments or information
